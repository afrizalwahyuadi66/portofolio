# **App Name**: Synthweave PortFolio

## Core Features:

- Cinematic Scroll Navigation: Implement smooth, scroll-driven animations and transitions between distinct sections of the CV, creating a narrative flow.
- Interactive Profile Sections: Dynamically display pentester experience, skills, and projects using responsive React components that can feature hover effects or expanding details.
- Floating Animated UI Elements: Incorporate subtle, performance-optimized floating elements or particles in the background to enhance the cinematic and modern feel.
- Fully Responsive Design: Ensure the entire landing page and its animations adapt flawlessly across various screen sizes, from mobile devices to large desktops.
- Direct Contact & Social Links: Prominently feature professional contact methods (email, phone) and links to relevant platforms like LinkedIn and GitHub.
- CV Download Functionality: Provide a clear call to action for visitors to download the full CV in a standard document format (e.g., PDF).

## Style Guidelines:

- The visual journey begins with a sophisticated, deep violet as the primary color. It's vibrant enough to captivate attention without overpowering, symbolizing modern tech and elegance (Primary: HSL(260, 70%, 55%) -> #8538F0).
- The canvas for this cinematic experience is a background of extreme darkness with just a whisper of the primary's violet hue, providing a profound depth essential for the scroll choreography (Background: HSL(260, 15%, 10%) -> #1B151F).
- A crisp, radiant blue serves as the accent, providing stark contrast and highlighting interactive elements or key information against the darker palette (Accent: HSL(230, 80%, 65%) -> #7AACFF).
- Headline font: 'Space Grotesk' (sans-serif) to convey a technical, modern, and precise aesthetic that complements the pentester theme.
- Body font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for optimal readability and a neutral, objective feel for detailed professional content, pairing effectively with the bold headlines.
- Utilize minimalist, geometric icons with clean lines that subtly allude to cybersecurity, data, and digital precision, integrated sparingly for emphasis rather than distraction.
- Sections are designed for full-viewport takeover with precise alignment of content, allowing scroll choreography to reveal elements cleanly and dramatically, much like cinematic scenes.
- Implement fluid, hardware-accelerated animations for scroll-triggered content reveals, subtle parallax effects on background elements, and gentle, performance-optimized floating motions to enhance the dynamic feel without compromising speed.
